#4 luqiu...@gmail.com 
解决了这个问题，把proxy.ini中的
scholar.google.com = google_cn,nocrlf,noforcehttps,nofakehttps
scholar.google.com.hk = google_cn,nocrlf,noforcehttps,nofakehttps
scholar.google.com.cn = google_cn,nocrlf,noforcehttps,nofakehttps
换成
scholar.google.com = google_hk,nocrlf,noforcehttps,nofakehttps
scholar.google.com.hk = google_hk,nocrlf,noforcehttps,nofakehttps
scholar.google.com.cn = google_hk,nocrlf,noforcehttps,nofakehttps
即可。
