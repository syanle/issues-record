用 chrome 打开 https://xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx 会告诉你答案的（默认隐藏，需要点开一个链接）。

This server could not prove that it is 173.194.14.78; its security certificate is from *.c.docs.google.com. This may be caused by a misconfiguration or an attacker intercepting your connection.

This server could not prove that it is 64.15.113.90; its security certificate is from *.googlevideo.com. This may be caused by a misconfiguration or an attacker intercepting your connection.

看到 *.c.docs.google.com／*.googlevideo.com 吧，显然这两个跟谷歌搜索的域名有一定的差距，事实证明，这两种是不适用于谷歌搜索(或YouTube)的。

不过像这样手动一个一个IP检查显然是很麻烦的。
