无法连接到真正的 twitter.com

您与 twitter.com 之间的安全连接目前正受到干扰。

请等待几分钟后再尝试重新加载网页，或在切换到其他网络后重新加载网页。如果您最近曾连接到新的 Wi-Fi 网络，请先登录再重新加载。

如果您想要立即访问 twitter.com，您的私人信息可能会泄露给攻击者。为了保护您的隐私，Chrome 在与真正的 twitter.com 建立安全连接之前，将不会加载该网页。



twitter.com normally uses encryption (SSL) to protect your information. When Chrome tried to connect to twitter.com this time, twitter.com returned unusual and incorrect credentials. Either an attacker is trying to pretend to be twitter.com, or a Wi-Fi sign-in screen has interrupted the connection. Your information is still secure because Chrome stopped the connection before any data was exchanged.

Network errors and attacks are usually temporary, so this page will probably work later. You can also try switching to another network.
