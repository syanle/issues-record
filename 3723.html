On March 28 we released version 1.6.4 of the Go App Engine SDK. The
new SDK is based on Go 1, and includes some backwards-incompatible
changes to the Go language and standard libraries and the App Engine
API. The new SDK has an api_version of 'go1'.

With that release we deprecated api_version 3. Apps that use
api_version 3 will stop working after May 31 2012.

Your Google Account "XXXX@gmail.com" is associated with the following
api_version 3 app(s):

    ixxxmmm version 1

Please update to api_version go1 and re-deploy before May 31 2012 to
avoid downtime.

The gofix tool packaged with the SDK can assist in updating your code.
