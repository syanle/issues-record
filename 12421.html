#1 ericcl...@gmail.com 
You have so many questions. I'll try to share some of my own observation:
Yes, google_cn is permanently restricted, or at least it will be like this in the foreseeable future. Reason unknown. And yes, only servers in other countries could be used(not limited to hk, though, any other country will work). My guess is that gfw mostly works on border routes so it is difficult to detect such traffic inside China. Therefore someone powerful enough somehow persuaded google to block appspot access to China servers. Or it could be google trying to reduce its bandwidth cost. I honestly don't know.

Also time could be another factor. Generally speaking, speed is better in late night, say, after 1am, 2am, if you can stay up.

If your main headache comes from Youtube, you can install 'youtube center' and in 'settings', turn off 'dash playback'. It will force the player to stream the whole video instead of chunks of it. This works better with goagent.
